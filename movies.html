Movie Reviews


Arrival

January 2022's Movie of the Month

A linguist works with the military to communicate with alien lifeforms after twelve mysterious spacecraft appear around the world.

Director: Denis Villenauve

Lead Actress: Amy Adams

Year of Release: 2016

Length of the movie: 1hr 56m

Maturity Rating: 12A

Review: This film displayed the skill of an intelligent director touching upon the complex topic that is 'extraterrestrial life'. The sheer brilliance of the concept and thought provoking ideas, were beautifully captured within this movie as a whole. We as a club agree that we were captivated by this film, beginning to end. This film is not your regular alien invasion action thriller, with gore and destruction. This movie offers a more philosophical approach to how our world could react to such a unique invasion. We highly recommend this film.

Edge Of Tomorrow

February 2022's Movie of the Month

A soldier fighting aliens gets to relive the same day over and over again, the day restarting every time he dies.

Director: Doug Liman

Lead Actor: Tom Cruise

Year of Release: 2014

Length of the movie: 1hr 53m

Maturity Rating: 12

Review: On the surface, one could assume this movie was just another Tom Cruise action packed movie that lacks plot but has a lot of cool explosions and car chases. We were sorely mistaken, this movie provided a wonderful amount of suspense and curiosity. The CGI in the movie was executed well, the script was well thought out. The reason we have not rated this movie any higher is because the final moments of the movie show to be a bit ridiculous. However, we appreciate that this is fiction and still thought it was a satisfactory way to bring the film to an end.
