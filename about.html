About Us


We are a Brighton based movie club with a passion from space-themed Science-fiction films.



WHO ARE WE?

We are mainly a collective of local Brighton residents. In 2021, 5 friends founded Beam Me Up! Since then we have grown to over 20 members!


WHAT IS OUR CLUB?

We are a movie club who specifically watch space-themed Science-fiction films. If you couldn't tell, our movie club's name gives a nod to the beloved classic, Star Trek. We felt this name fitted perfectly as we two would like to be beamed up into the immersive experience that is Cinema. And explore the unknown depicted in many space-themed sci-fi movies.


WHEN AND HOW DO WE MEET?

  1. We organise in person meet ups at either people's houses/ local cinema's.

  2. We sometimes, watch the movies in smaller groups/ by ourselves.

  3. Once we have all watched the film of the month you can email your review to the head of the club, Megan Horsley.

HOW DOES THE MOVIE REVIEW PORCESS WORK?

Once the head of the club, Megan has recieved all the reviews, which includes a rating out of 5. She will create an average and provide a review which best reflects the club's overall view.


DO I HAVE TO BE A BRIGHTON LOCAL

If you are able to attend the in person meet ups on a regular occasion then anyone is welcome.
